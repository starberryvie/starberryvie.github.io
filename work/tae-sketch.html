Taehyung - Sketch

This is a sketch of Kim Taehyung of BTS, a famous Korean boy group. Here I tried to focus on the details in his hair, glasses, and clothing. This guy is known for his playful, naive behavior, so I tried to keep these characteristics of his personality while adding character to the drawing by using only thin strokes without blending. This sketch is done on Adobe Illustrator Draw on the iPad.
