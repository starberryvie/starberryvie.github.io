Ballerina Series

Being a ballet dancer means having an utmost dedication to this six-century old art form. I wanted to portray the emotion ballet dancers invest in order to get into character and reveal the pain, hardships, and the stress on the mind and body, while still being elegant with highly technical form. This series of ballerina pieces is made with silver scratchboard.
