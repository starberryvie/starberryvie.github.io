John Lennon

This a poster of John Lennon, one of the most respected musicians in history. The background features John Lennon’s billboard of “War is Over!” at the Times Square, where I changed the bottom two rows of text into his name and years alive and added The Beatles’ logo on the other side. I put a picture of John Lennon sitting on an oversized guitar (with his signature) since psychedelia plays a large role in John Lennon’s work.
